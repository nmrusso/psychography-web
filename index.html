02 — The Band

From darkness
to the light.

Psychography was born as a band project in 2020 in the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina, founded by Yanina Islas (Vocals). Their songs reflect a blend of different metal subgenres, creating a progressive-tinged atmosphere that moves between Metalcore and Thall.

With five singles released, a feature on Under The Wing by Dan Sugarman (Ice Nine Kills), and being the first Argentine band on the Arson Theory distributor, Psychography has consolidated its sonic identity.

In 2026 they premiered the official music video for Screams In The Mind.
